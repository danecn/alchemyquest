About

Alchemy Quest is an HTML5 game.

The goal of this game is to be the best.

You have align minimum 3 items vertically or horizontally to win points.

When you arrive to a specific amount of points your level is increased. You win more points and time decrease faster.

There are 3 modes of difficulty.
